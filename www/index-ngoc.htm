Facebook Re-connect

Ever felt disconnected with an old friend and wanted to catch up? Wanted to see how high school or college friends from way back are doing? Facebook Re-connect allows you to do just that! Simply log in to Facebook, enter a friend's name and an amount of time to see what they've been up to. If it's been extremely long, don't worry. You'll only get important events in their life. Only pictures and status without the most comments and likes will be shown. You'll also be able to see the mutual friends that you two may have befriended over the years. Log in in the upper right corner to start!
